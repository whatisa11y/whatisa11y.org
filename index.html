a11y

  1. A short way of writing "accessibility."
  2. A numeronym of "accessibility" created by replacing the middle eleven letters with the number 11. (a + 11 + y)

See also: "a11y" according to The A11y Project.

So, what is "accessibility?"

Accessibility is many things. When "a11y" is used, it usually means the practice of making websites, applications, and other programs usable by people with disabilities.
